This game consists of three types of questions.

1. Yes/No questions can be answered with any of the following:
	Yes can be entered as either "Yes" or "Y"
	No can be entered as either "No" or "N"
2. The game may ask you for a number of rounds.  This choice must be a number.
3. When asked to "Choose your weapon!" you may pick any of the following:
	"Rock" or "R"
	"Paper" or "P"
	"Scissors" or "S"

Don't worry, these choices aren't case-sensitive!

Once you have finished reading the instructions, press any key to continue.
